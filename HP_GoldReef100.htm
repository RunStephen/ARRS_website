Notes: 1982 was the inaugural year. The 1982 race was from Ventersdorp to Roodepoort; the 1983 and 1984 races were from Roodepoort to Pretoria; the 1985-1990 races were from Pretoria to Roodepoort. An out-and-back course from Maraisburg was adopted in 1991 and later, the race start and finish were moved to the permanent clubhouse of the organizing club, located in Roodepoort, again with course variations from year to year. In 2006, the start was moved to Hartebeesport Dam (1132m elevation) but still finishing at the clubhouse (1750m elevation).
