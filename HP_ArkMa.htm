Notes: 1968 was the inaugural year. The venue was Searcy for 1968-1969; Petit Jean Mountain (Morrilton) for 1970-75 and 1977; Little Rock Air Force Base for 1976, Booneville for 1979-1996 (out-and-back, uncertified course), and Malvern for 1997-2002. The 2003-2010 races were held on an out-and-back course, starting and finishing in Benton. The race was known as the Ground Hog Day Marathon for 1968-1977 and was held on the Saturday nearest Ground Hog Day (February 2nd).  Times for 2017 are net times.
