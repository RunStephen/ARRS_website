Notes: This race has undergone several name changes. It was known as "Tangerine Bowl" for the 1977-82 races, "Citrus Bowl" for the 1983-1991 races, "Citrus Sports" for the 1992-1994 and 1996-1997 races, Burger King Classic for the 1995 race, Shaw Contract Flooring for the 1998 race, and Orlando Utilities Commission for the 1999 race. The 1984 course was 69m short.

(nc) => national championship

1988 Mark Stickley was the national champion (2nd place, 1:02:42)
