Notes: 1981 was the inaugural year. For 1981-2006, the course was point-to-point, from Junee to Wagga Wagga.  The subsequent course has been a loop course around Wagga Wagga.  The 1981 race was known as the Olympic Way Marathon, the 1982-2003 races were known as the Junee to Wagga Wagga Marathon, the 2004-2006 races were known as the Riverina Marathon.
