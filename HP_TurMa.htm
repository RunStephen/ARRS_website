Notes: 1974 was the inaugural year. The 1991-1997 races started in Avigliana and finished in Turin. The 1978-1979 races started in Turin and finished in Sommariva del Bosco and were known as the Maratona di Sommariva del Bosco. The 1987-1990 races started in Susa and finished in Avigliana and were known as the Susavigliana Marathon. The 1982 race was short by an estimated 2-3 km; The 1987 race distance was 40.5 km; the 1991 race distance was 41.45 km or 745 m short. Elena Dupono was reported as the first ITA woman to break three hours for a marathon.
