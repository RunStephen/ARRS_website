Notes: 1971 was the inaugural year. This race was known as the Oil Capital Marathon for 1971-1979, the Will Rogers Oil Capital Marathon for 1980-1983, the Tulsa Marathon for 1986-1997, the Oklahoma Invitational Marathon for 1998-1999, the Oklahoma Marathon for 2000-date. The 1998 race was officially cancelled but several runners came and ran the course anyway. This race was replaced by the Route 66 Marathon but since both races were held in 2006, the Route 66 Marathon cannot be considered a continuation of this series.
