Notes: 1976 was the inaugural year. The course climbs from 67m in Pistoia to 1388m in Abetone (24.9 m/km) on paved roads. Courses for 2008 and earlier were not measured by the Italian federation and so the exact distance is uncertain.  The course was certified at 50 km in 2007.  Prior to 2007, the course was about 52 km except for the 1980 course that was about 49 km.
