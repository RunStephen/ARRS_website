Notes: 1976 was the inaugural year. This event was known the Pepsi Marathon for 1986 and was the RRCA Championships. It was known as the Trustmark Phidippides Marathon from 1988 thru 1990 and as the Mississippi Marathon for all other years prior to 2008.. For 1978-1980, the race started in Natchez Trace and finished in Clinton; for 1981-1984, the race was held in Jackson; for 1985-87 and 1991-2007 the race was held in Clinton; for 1988-1990 the race was held in Madison.  The 2011 race was the RRCA championships.
