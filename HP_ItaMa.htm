Salvatore Cuccuru (ITA)
Notes: 1962 was the inaugural year. For 1997, the race was run from Reggio Emilia to Carpi. For 1999 to 2011 and 2013-date, the race has been run from Maranello to Carpi, a distance of 28 km, a drop of 2.4 m/km.  The race started and finished in Carpi for all other years. The 1988 race is considered as a test run by the present organizers; the distance was about 41.9 km. Races prior to 1988 were organized by a different group than at present.. The 1969 course was approximately 41 km.  This race was also known as the Italia Memorial Enzo Ferrari Marathon. In 2016, Jaouad Zain (MAR) finished first in 2:23:57 but was later disqualified for drug use.
