Notes: 1973 was the inaugural year. The 1982 race was postponed due to inclement weather and was held on 10 Apr 1983. This race was known as the Maryland Marathon and was run from Memorial Stadium to Loch Raven Reservoir and return thru 1979. The 1980 race was run from Memorial Stadium to the Civic Center. For 1981, the race was renamed the Baltimore City Marathon and a new city-wide course was run. The 1988 and 1989 editions were run along the Inner Harbor.
