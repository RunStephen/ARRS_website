Notes: 1980 was the inaugural year. The course thru 1994 was point-to-point, starting 42 km south of Salmon at 4392 feet (1339 m) elevation, following Highway 93 and finishing in Salmon and an elevation of 3945 feet (1202 m, drop 3.2 m/km).  The 2008-date races have started in Tendoy, 32 km southeast of Salmon at 4881 feet (1488 m) elevation and have finished at 3931 feet (1198m, drop 6.87 m/km). This race was held as part of the Salmon River Days Festival and was known as the Salmon River Summer Marathon and the Salmon River Days Marathon thru 1994.
