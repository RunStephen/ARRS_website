Notes: The inaugural year was 1974. This race was known as the Skylon International Marathon for 1974-1982, the Buffalo Niagara Falls International Marathon for 1983-1986, the Niagara Falls Marathon in 1997 and the Casino Niagara International Marathon for 1998-2008. In the 1985 race, runners were led off course early, several routes were used by runners to get back on course plus other problems, making times/places rather unreliable.
