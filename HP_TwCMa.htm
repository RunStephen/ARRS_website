Notes: 1963 was the inaugural year; the race was known as the River Roads. This event was known as the Land of Lakes for 1964-66 and the City of Lakes Marathon for 1967-81 On 04 Oct 1981, the St Paul Marathon was also held (won by Michael Seaman {MN/USA} in 2:20:36 and Maggie Munro {USA} in 3:06:08.. In 1982, these two marathons merged to form the Twin Cities Marathon, starting in Minneapolis and finishing in St Paul. The 1964 course was found to have been 27m200y (43.635 km) on remeasurement using a calibrated wheel.
