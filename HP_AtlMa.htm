Notes: An informal race was held in March 1963 with four finishers, won by Billy Daniel (GA/USA) and Dale Smith (GA/USA) in 3:32:23. This race is not considered part of the series. The Atlanta Marathon was held on a double loop course from 1963 thru 1980 when it was moved to a downtown venue. From 1984 thru 1991, the race was held from Lithonia into Atlanta (pt/pt). The 1992 thru 1996 races were held on a loop course starting and finishing near the Atlanta Fulton County Stadium. The 1997 and subsequent races have been held on a course closely following the 1996 Olympic Marathon course. The race was also known as the Peach Bowl Marathon thru 1975.
