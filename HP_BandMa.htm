Notes: 1983 was the inaugural year. In 1996, the race was postponed one week because of the Dome wildfire; in 2000, the race was delayed five months because of the Cerro Grange wildfire. The start for all years has been the Nazarene Church in White Rock. For 1983-87, the finish was at Piñon Park in White Rock; for 1988-2004, the race finished in the starting area. The course was first certified in 1991.
