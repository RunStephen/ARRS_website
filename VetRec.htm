All-Time Veterans Rankings

This section lists the all-time best performances for the five year age groups, 40-44, 45-49, 50-54,....., for both men and women.  Ages are calculated based on reported dates of birth.  Only performances with ages documented by dates of birth are included.  Lists are generally intended to be 100 deep for the major events (10K, half marathon, marathon for road and 3000m, 5000m, and 10000m for outdoor track and 50 deep for all other events.  Qualifying standards represent the last ranked performance for completed lists (100 or 50 deep) or are otherwise based on an algorithm designed to produce a consistent set of standards over the range of distances and age groups.
