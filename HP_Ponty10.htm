Notes: 1984 was the inaugural year. The race is called the Reverse 10 because a marshalling error in its inaugural year caused all the runners to run the course in the wrong direction. In recognition of that fact, the mile markers since have always been displayed in reverse order!  The 2010 and subsequent races include some off-road portions.
