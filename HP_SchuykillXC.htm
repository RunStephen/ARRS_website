Notes: 1899 was the inaugural year. The 1945 course was shorter, to Meade's Monument and return. The 1952 race was run as a handicap.  The first male and female finishers are given, when known.  For some years, guest runners were allowed and only the first Schuylkill Navy member is known.  The first and last 1.5 miles are on roads with about 2 miles of crosscountry for the middle portion of the race.

Note on dates: Thanksgiving was the last Thursday in November for 1863-1938, the third Thursday in November for 1939-1941, and has been the fourth Thursday in November since then.
