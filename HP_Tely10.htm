Notes: 1922 was the inaugural year. The course starts in Paradise and finishes at Bannerman Park in the heart of Saint John's. Different routes have been used over the years. Originally named the Telegram 10 mile, the race is more popularly known as the Tely Ten. In recent years, it has been known as the Evening Telegram 10 mile. The 1922 course was remeasured in 1923 and found to have been 459 yards (420 meters) short. The 1948 race was postponed from 15 Sep 1948 due to hurricane winds; the 1976 race was rescheduled from 16 May 1976 due to high winds.
