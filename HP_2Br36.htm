Notes: 1968 was the inaugural year. The race distance was 36m158y (58.082 km) for 1968-1995 and 35m498y (56.783 km) for 1996-date. For 2004 and 2005, the race was known as the Two Bridges Multi-Terrain Challenge and was 12 miles trail, 9 miles track and 14 miles road.  The original course started at the main entrance to Dunfermline Glen, and went through Crossford, Torryburn and Culross, over the Kincardine Bridge, and then on to Skinflats, Bo’ness, Newton, over the Forth Road Bridge, up to Inverkeithing and finished at Rosyth Civil Service Sports Centre.
