Notes: 1958 was the inaugural year. Race starts at Meadowbank in Edinburgh and follows the coastal route to North Berwick, finishing at the North Berwick Post Office. On 09 Dec 1953, Joe McGhee ran the 22.875 mile (36.8 km) course from Edinburgh to North Berwick in 2:05:19.6 as a time trial (not a race). This bettered a previous record of 2:38:15 set by J Morrow (SCO) in 1930.
