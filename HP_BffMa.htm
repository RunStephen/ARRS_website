Notes: 1974 was the inaugural year. The course from Macleantown to East London drops approximately 400 metres from start to finish or about 9.5 m/km. For 1984 and 1985 a slightly different, but still downhill, course was used due to road construction. The 1986 course was an out-and-back route at sea level. The race finishes at the Beacon Bay Country Club and is the same finish as used for the Border Masters Marathon.  The 2016 course was a certified standard course.
