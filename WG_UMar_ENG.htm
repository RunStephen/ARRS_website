Ultramarathon Working Group

This group focuses on races beyond the marathon distance. Records and rankings are kept for the following standard distances: 30 miles, 50km, 40 miles, 50 miles, 100km, 100 miles, 200km, 24 hours, 48 hours, 6 days, and 1000 miles.


Goals

To collect and disseminate information and results of ultra races.

To keep and maintain deep ranking lists for the international standard events of 100km and 24 hours.

To keep and maintain ranking lists for the other standard distances.

To keep and maintain ultra world records for road and track standard events.

To track and maintain lists for long running ultra marathons.
