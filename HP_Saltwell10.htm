Notes: The inaugural year was 1911. The race distance for 1911-1938 was 6.75 miles (10.86 km); 6.25 miles for 1945-1984, and in 2000 was 9 km.  This race was known as the Boxing Day Open Road Races thru 1969.  Prior to 1987, a women's race was held irregularly at distances of 1.5 or 2 miles (details are not known).  The 2001 race was postponed due to bad weather.
