Notes: 1982 was the inaugural year. For 1982 thru 1990, the race went from Taylor Wine Park in Hammondsport to Hodgman Park in Painted Post; for 1991 thru 1994, the race went from Hodgman Park in Painted Post to Taylor Wine Park in Hammondsport; for 1995 and later, the race went from Philips Lighting in Bath to Corning Glass Museum in Corning. All courses are point to point with roughly 29km separation between the start and finish.
