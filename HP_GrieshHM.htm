Notes: 1971 was the inaugural year. The race distance was 25 km for 1971-1987 for both men and women. For 1988-1992, the men continued with the 25 km distance while the women ran 15 km. The 25 km course was a flat, out-and-back course; the current half marathon course is a flat three loops.  The 2012 race was held before the German championships race, starting at 9:30 am viz 12:30 pm for the championships.
