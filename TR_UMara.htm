Ultramarathon Trivia

Oldest combined ages for men/women winners in same race. The Self Transcendence 24 hour, held London ENG on 11-12 Oct 2003 was won by Don Ritchie (06 Jul 1944, SCO) iwith 189.163 km at age 59y 97d for the men and by Sandra Brown (01 Apr 1949, GBR) with 179.842 km at age 54y193d for the women. Their combined ages are 113y290d.  The undocumented (no dates of birth) combination of  Don Winkley (age 62) and Cassandra Johnson (age 54) at the Across the Years (AZ/USA) 72 Hr on 29 Dec 2000 with a combined age in excess of 116 years has been reported.
