Road Race Trivia

The largest number of runners in a declared tie for 1st place. On 13 Jan 1979, a six-way tie for first place was declared at the Governor's Cup (Salem OR/USA) Half Marathon with all runners being given the time of 1:04:46. The winners (in alphabetical order) were Doug Brown, Thom Burleson, Alex Kasich, Ralph King, Mike Manke, and Kevin McCarey.

Runners who have won a race in a town of the same name. Only two examples have been found. Lucio Fregona (ITA) won a marathon in Fregona ITA on 18 Sep 2004, running a 3:35:51. Helen Clitheroe won a 10K in Clitheroe ENG on 27 Dec 2004, running 32:30, again on 28 Dec 2008 running 32:31, and yet again on 27 Dec 2009, running 32:53..
