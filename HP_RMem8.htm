Notes: 1973 was the inaugural year. This race was known as the New Year's Eve Race for 1973-78; the Chris McKinnon Memorial Race for 1979 until the 1990's when the name was changed to its present name to commemorate all Western Cape runners who have passed away. In 1978 and 1989, the race was held on December 30th to avoid holding the race on a Sunday.
