Notes: 1929 was the inaugural year. In 1991, the World Student Games Marathon (b) was run in conjunction with the Sheffield Marathon. This event was known as the Sheffield Independent Marathon for 1929-1931,as the Doncaster to Sheffield Marathon for 1946-1961, and the Sheffield Marathon since 1982. The 1946-61 races were all held on the Monday following Easter Sunday. Only the half marathon was run in 2004 and later years.
