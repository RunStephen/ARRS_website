08 Jan 1898 2m297y --- P J Lonergan (IRL)
Notes: 1898 was the inaugural year. For 1927-1933, a ten mile race was held in lieu of the invitational run.  A race was possibly held on 11 Feb 1939.  Distances are as reported. The course starts in Finglass Village (elevation ca 200' MSL) and proceeds southeast, reaching a low point of 73' MSL near half way, then climbing to 105' MSL, and finishing at an elevation of ca 84' MSL.  The net drop is about 11 m/km.  Races apparently were held for women for 1964-1969 at a distance of 1320y and for 1970-1984 at a distance of 1 mile.
