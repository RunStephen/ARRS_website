Notes: 1908 was the inaugural year. The finish was at Crispin Field for 1946-1954 and at the YMCA thereafter. This race was originally named the Berwick Marathon but changed its name to the Run for the Diamonds in the early 1980's.

Note on dates: Thanksgiving was the last Thursday in November for 1863-1938, the third Thursday in November for 1939-1941, and has been the fourth Thursday in November since then.
