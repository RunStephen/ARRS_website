Notes: 1965 was the inaugural year. It is not known when the race switched from December to January but at least one calendar year between1965 and 1972 did not have a race.  The "missing" year is tentatively set as 1966.  The 1972 course was reported as 12m40y (19.349 km).  The 1973 and 1975 courses are also considered to have been short.  This race was held at Hains Point in Washington DC thru 2005.  Only the 5K was held in 2006 due to permitting problems with the National Park Service.  The race was held at an alternate location in Washington DC for 2007-2011 before moving to Carderock MD in 2012.
