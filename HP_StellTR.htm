Notes: 1989 was the inaugural year. The men's race starts at 500m and finishes at 1960m. The women's race starts at 1375m and also finishes at 1960m. The 1989-1991 races were team races.  The 2009 race was held on a slightly different course.  The 2010 distances were 11.03 km for men and 7.67 km for women.  For 2011-date, both men and women ran 11.03 km.  This race was known as the Challenge Stellina thru 2006.  In 2016, a separate masters race was held, won by Cesar Costa (POR) in 1:08:53 (women's winner unknown).
