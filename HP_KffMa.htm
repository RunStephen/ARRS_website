Notes: 1964 was the inaugural year. For 1995 and earlier, this event was known as the Kaffrarian Marathon. The 1968 race only had three entrants and was not held. The course used through the 2003 race started in Stutterheim and finished in King William's Town, dropping roughly 350 meters from start to finish (8.3 m/km). The 2004 and subsequent races have been held on a near loop course (S/F separation ca 10 km).
