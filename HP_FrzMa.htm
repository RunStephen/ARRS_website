Notes: 1984 was the inaugural year. The 1984 course was about 41.23 km or about 965 m short.  The 2001-2009 courses had a net drop of 56 m (Piazzale Michelangelo at 104 m => Piazza San Cross 48 m) altho the start and finish were separated by only 750 meters.  Previous courses had a drop of about 10 meters and the start and finish were about 50m to 60m apart.  The 2010-2011 courses had a drop of 40 meters, within the allowable 1 m/km drop for record quality courses.  The 2012-date course have a start/finish separation of 700 meters, from 50m to 48m elevation.
