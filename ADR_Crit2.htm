Criteria for Inclusion in the ARRS Database

All performances by time qualified runners are eligible for inclusion in the ARRS database. A runner is time qualified if he/she meets any one of the performance standards listed below. 

Runners who do not qualify on the basis of time may qualify as "other" if they meet one or more of the following standards: (1) if he/she wins a single prize purse of US$1000 or more, (2) if he/she wins a marathon, or (3) if he/she meets one or more of the time qualifying standards for veterans (40+) (www.arrs.net/VetRec.htm). Peformances by other qualified runners are included in the database if (1) the distance is marathon or longer, (2) if they win prize money of any amount, (3) if they win a national championship, or (4) if they post a gun time in a standard distance race on a certified course and are over age 40 at the time of the performance.
