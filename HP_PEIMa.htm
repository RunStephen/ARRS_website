Notes: 1979 was the inaugural year. Known as the PEI Roadrunners Marathon for 1979-1984 and the Island Marathon for 1985-2003. For 1979-1984 and 2004-date, the race was run from Cavendish to Charlottetown, a separation of 33.6 km (point-to-point). In 2004, an alternate race known as the Stratford Marathon was held on 18 Sep; the race was won by David Forsythe (CAN) in 3:13:33 and by Pam Power-McKenna (CAN) in 3:22:17.
