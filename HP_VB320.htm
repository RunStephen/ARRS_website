Notes: 1990 was the inaugural year. The route and distance has varied over the years.  Prior to 2006, the first stage was Vienna AUT => Sopron HUN, a distance of about 94 km and the second stage was Sopron HUN to Györ HUN, a distance of about 116 km.  For 2006 and later, the first stage was Vienna AUT => Bratislava SVK, a distance of about 92 km and the second stage was Bratislava SVK => Györ HUN a distance of about 84 km.  The third thru fifth stages have remained the same with the third stage being Györ HUN => Tata HUN, a distance of about 61 km, the fourth stage being Tata HUN => Budakeszi HUN, a distance of about 60 km, and the final stage being a half marathon from Budakeszi HUN => Budapest HUN.  The half marathon for more recent years has been an open race with participants who were not part of the stage race.  Prior to 2006, the combined distance was about 350 km; for 2006 and later, the combined distance was about 320 km.  The 2010 stage race was cancelled; only the half marathon was held.
