Notes: 1971 was the inaugural year. The 1971-1988 races were known as the Juneau Marathon with each race starting at the Mendenhall Glacier but several different courses were used over the years.  These races were sponsored by the Juneau Parks & Recreation Department.  The race course was first certified in 1992.  The 1992 race was named in honor of Frank Maier, a local marathon enthusiast, who died in a fishing accident in 1990.
