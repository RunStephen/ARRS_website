Monte Wolford (CO/USA)
Notes: 1956 was the inaugural year. For the first four years, the start and finish were at the Cog Rail Station. In 1960 the finish was moved to the corner of Ruxton and Manitou, extending the course by 1.3 km. In 1976 the start was moved from the Cog Rail Station to the city hall, further extending the course by 1.9 km. Starting in 1981, the ascent and round-trip races were held on separate days.
