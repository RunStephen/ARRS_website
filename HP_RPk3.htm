Notes: 1893 was the inaugural race. The climb is 700 feet (210 meters). The 1893-1902 races started and finished at the recreation grounds; the 1929-39 races started at the Crown Hotel; the 1947 and subsequent races started from the Pickup and Crankshaw sportsfield until 1956 when the start was at the beginning of Lever Park Avenue and was held on Easter Saturday.  The 1946 and 1947 races were known as the WWII Victory Run or the War Memorial Gala.  Distances are approximate; the 1902 race was perhaps 400-500 meters longer.
