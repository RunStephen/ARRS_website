Notes: 1978 was the inaugural year. The race was initially called the Schipholloop and was about 59 km. The course was later lengthen to 61 km for most of the runnings and the last two runnings, the distances was 62.5 km. The race name was later changed to Ronde van de Haarlemmeermeer. The course was one large loop along the dike around the polder Haarlemmermeer.
