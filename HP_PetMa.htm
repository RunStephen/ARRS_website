Course Information

1935.... => Three loop course starting and finishing on the high school track
1936-41 => Point-to-point course from Cunningham Station, southeast of Sebastopol, through Santa Rosa, and finishing in Petaluma
1942-54 => Three loop course, starting and finishing at Kenilworth Park
1955-59 => Five loop course, starting and finishing at the Old Adobe on Washington St

Note: These results were preserved by Edward Fratini in the Petaluma Argus-Courier and elsewhere.
