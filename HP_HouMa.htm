Notes: The race was known for many years until 2000 as the Methodist Health Care Houston Marathon, 2001-2002 as the Compaq Houston Marathon, 2003-2005 as the HP Houston Marathon and 2006 to date as the Chevron Houston Marathon.The 1980 course was about 800 meters short. The 1979 course has also been reported short by an unspecified amount. The 1992 race was also the USA Olympic Trials for women.

disqualifications:  In 1996, Tatiana Pozdniakova (UKR) was first across the finish line with a time of 2:31:03 but was subsequently disqualified after testing positive for pseudoephedrine and ephedrine, substances found in cold medications.  In 2016, Gebo Burka Gameda (ETH) was first male with a time of 2:10:54 but failed an in-competition drug test. Girmay Birhanu Gebru (ETH), second in 2:11:05, was subsequently disqualified for use of Meldonium.
