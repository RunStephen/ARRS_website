Previous Year Rankings

This section lists the fastest times for each standard distance for the previous year. The current year rankings are listed under "Current Year Rankings." Previous year rankings may not reflect the actual previous year, in which case, refer to the current year rankings as there have not been sufficient numbers of quality performances to warrant a ranking for the current year. This transition is generally complete by the end of June each year. Rankings are terminated if they reach the cut-off performance for automatic inclusion in the ADR database before the desired number of performances is reached.
