Notes: 1975 was the inaugural year. This race was held on Wednesdays thru 1991. The 1987 course was long. For 1975-1990, the course was two loops with the start and finish in Hoek vanHolland; for 1991 to date, the course was one small loop and two larger loops with the start and finish in Maasdijk.  This race was known as the Jan Knijnenburgloop 15 km thru the 2009 running.  The race distance prior to 2012 was 15 km.
