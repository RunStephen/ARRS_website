Notes: 1993 was the inaugural year.  The men's and women's elite races for 2002 and 2012 were held on separate days with popular (citizens) races held in conjunction (day 1- women's elite, men's popular; day 2- men's elite, women's popular).  The winners of the popular races: 2002 men 3:22:51.9 Markus Kellenberger (SUI) and women 4:07:26.7 Rita Born (SUI); 2012 men 3:37:02.2 Adam Gröbli (SUI) women 4:32:49.2 Monica Zehnder (SUI).  The course is on mountain trails, starting at Interlaken (566m elevation), peaking at Eigerletscher (2205m) and finishing at Kleine Scheidegg (2100m).  For 2016-2017 and probably for several earlier years, the times were net times.  Starting in 2017, an "optimized," i.e., faster, course has been used.
