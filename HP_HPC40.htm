Notes: 1972 was the inaugural year. This is a team race with all four team members completing the course together. Individual team members' names are not available; only team names are shown. The race is run at night in the winter. Times vary markedly due to the weather and course conditions. The "bad weather route" was followed in 1986-87 and 1998. The 1999 race was shortened due to bad weather. A slightly different course was introduced in 2004.
