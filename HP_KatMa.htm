Kiyoshi Shinozaki (JPN)
Notes: 1953 was the inaugural year. Thru 1960, this race was known as the Yomiuri Zenkoku Marathon. For 1953-1954, the race was held in Tokyo, for 1955-1960 the race was held in Ome. It was then held in Utsunomiya for 1961 and in Mito for 1962-1969 before moving to Katsuta starting in 1970. In November 1994, the towns of Katsuta and Nakaminato were merged and took on the name Hitachinaka. Before 1984, the first women finisher is listed; the first official women's winner was in 1984.
