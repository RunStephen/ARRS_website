Notes: The inaugural year was 1896. Race distance was probably close to 5 miles (8.047 km) thru 1995 altho was reported as 5.2 miles for 1976-1977. This event was run as a handicap thru 1971 (fastest time is listed as the winner). The course is point-to-point. The race was known as the JY Cameron Memorial Turkey Trot thru 1997.

Note on dates: Thanksgiving was the last Thursday in November for 1863-1938, the third Thursday in November for 1939-1941, and has been the fourth Thursday in November since then.
