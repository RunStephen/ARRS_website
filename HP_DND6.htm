Leander Lennerton (NS/CAN)
Notes: 1907 was the inaugural year. The race distance in 1908 was 18 miles (29.0 km) and was from Halifax to Dartmouth. The 1920-21 races were for schoolboys; the distance was apparently about 3 miles. A new course was run for 1978 and later years. The race was known as the Evening Mail 6 mile from 1923. The women's race distance for 1972 was two miles; a 100 yd (91 m) race was held for women in 1967-1969 and possibly other years. The course was lengthed by about 200 yd (183 m) in the early 1970's.
