Runner Name Correspondence

These tables provide alternate family names that runners have been known by. For women, these alternate family names are often the result of marriage. Many Kenyan and Latin American runners have two "family" names and often appear in results with one or the other or sometimes both names. ADR tries to use the runner's most recently used name or most commonly used name in its lists.

These tables are sorted by most recent citizenship and then alphabetically by the alternate name. Complete male names are usually read as <given name> <family name> <alternate name>. For example, Aurelio Miti Handanga in ADR lists would be listed as Aurelio Miti.
