Notes: 1946 was the inaugural year.  The race distance for 1946-1963 was 10 miles.  The race was held in Tokyo for 1946-47 and 1949-1957; the race was held in or to Nerima for 1948 and 1958-1960; the race was run to Higashimurayama for 1961; the race was held in Edogawa for 1962-1963; the race was held in Tama for 1964; the race was held in Kemigawa for 1965-1970; the race was held in Hachioji for 1971-1976; the race was held on a multiple loop course in Oimachi for 1977-1999; the race has been held at Showa Kinen Park in Tachikawa since 2000 with the 2003 edition being a distance of 16.3 km and run partially on a dirt trail surface..
