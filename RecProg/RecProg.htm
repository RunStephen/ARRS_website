Record Progressions

Record progressions are shown to the extent known to ADR. Additions and corrections to these lists are always welcome (send to kcy@frontiernet.net). Road marks are limited to record quality marks (no point-to-point marks). A "+" suffixed to some 8km times indicates marks made at 5 miles. Progressions are generally not shown if the length of the progression known to ADR is one or less.
