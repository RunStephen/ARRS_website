Current Year Rankings

This section lists the fastest times for each standard distance for the current year. Last year's "final" rankings are listed under Previous Year Rankings. Rankings are terminated if they reach the cut-off performance for automatic inclusion in the ADR database before the desired number of performances is reached. Time, place in race, runner name, nationality and date of birth,and date and venue of the race are shown. Rankings for the following countries are included: ARG, AUS, AUT, BEL, BLR, BRA, BUL, CAN, CHN, COL, CRO, CUB, CZE, DEN, ECU, ESP, FIN, FRA, GBR, GER, GRE, HUN, IND, IRL, ISR, ITA, JPN, KOR, LTU, MEX, NED, NOR, NZL, PER, POL, POR, ROM, RSA, RUS, SER, SLO, SVK, SUI, SWE, TAN, TUR, UKR, VEN, ZIM, and USA. During the first few months of each year, these links may connect to the previous year's rankings until sufficient results are received to warrant posting a current year ranking.
