Notes: 1966 was the inaugural year. There were five starters in 1966. The race was known as the Greens-Winston Marathon for 1966-1974 and the Greensboro-Winston Salem Marathon for 1975,and the race was run from Greensboro to Winston-Salem. The 1983 race was known as the Greensboro Humana Marathon, the 1984 race was known as the Humana Hospital Greensboro Marathon, and the 1985 race was known as the Health America Marathon.
