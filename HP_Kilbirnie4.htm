Notes: 1928 was the inaugural year. The early races were ballot team (four person teams selected at random) crosscountry races.  The 1938 race was a team relay (fastest leg is given).  The 1939 event was a handicap race (fastest scratch time is given).  The 1949 and subsequent races were on roads.  Since 1990, the course has been four laps of a 1.16 mile loop.
