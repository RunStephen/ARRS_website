Notes:

Dates of birth are not reliable.

Michel Johann Théato competed for the St Mande AC, a French club based in Paris, but was born in Luxembourg.

The finish order for MacDonald and Grant never has been clarified with any certainty. Their times are based on a report that the race started at 2:36:37 and that "up to half past seven only seven had finished."

Zilia has been reported as being injured and did not start.

Countries were not "official" until 1906; runner's clubs are given where known.

The race distance was reported to be 40 km. The race opened with four laps on the 500m track at Bois de Boulogne, then traversed the Bois de Boulogne to Porte de Passy and on to the lap of Paris on the boulevard where the outer city wall had been, and returned to the track for a final three laps.
