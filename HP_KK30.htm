24 Jun 1989

Notes: 1989 was the inaugural year. The course follows the Baden Powell Trail from Horseshoe Bay (West Vancouver) to Deep Cove (North Vancouver) and is almost entirely run on trails with roughly 8000 feet (2440 meters) of ascent and 8300 feet (2530 meters) of descent.  The 1989 race was a group run with eight finishers, Louis Briere, David Brown, Tom Brown, Gavin Cameron, Shane Collins, Enzo Federico, Rob Hartvikson, and Brendan Kennelly.
