Notes: 1981 was the inaugural year.  The 1981-1985 races were held from Papara to Pirae on the island of Tahiti and were known as the Tahiti International Marathon. This race has been known as Moorea International (1988-1997), the Moorea Blue (1997-99), and the Tahiti Nui (2001-2006). The course starts and finishes in Papetoai with a turn-around just past Vaapiti, on the island of Moorea.  The 2014 course was about 40 km.
