Notes: 1978 was the inaugural year. For 1978-1998, the race was run from the Albany County Courthouse in Laramie to the State Capitol in Cheyenne, a distance of 50 miles. For 1999-2012, the race has been held on a double out-and-back course entirely within the Medicine Bow National Forest primarily on dirt and gravel roads.  This race has also been known as the Wyoming Double Marathon.  For 2013 and subsequent years, the race distance has been 50 km.
