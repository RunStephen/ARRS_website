Notes: 1980 was the inaugural year. This race was known as the Jelgavas Marathon for the first few years but was changed to the Ozolnieku Marathon since it starts and finishes in Ozolnieku LAT and its main organizer is the Ozolnieku Sports Club. The 1982 race was not held due to a ban by the governmental health services. Nadezda Kavtaskina was born 25 Jul 1936, making her 61 years old when she won in 1997.
