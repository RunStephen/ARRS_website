Notes: 1988 was the inaugural year. The race distance was 100 miles for 1988-1997. The 1988 race was held on the Westbourne Oval track, for 1989 it was held on a 2 km loop, for 1990-1995 it was held on a 1.6 km loop, for 1996-2003 it was held on a 2.3 km loop, for 2004-2006 it was held on a 2.5 km loop.  The race was known as the Algoa 100 mile/100 km for 1988-1999 and the Alan Alchin Memorial 100km for 2000-2006.  For 2007, it was renamed the Bay to Bay 100 km and held on a point-to-point course.
