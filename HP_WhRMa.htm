Notes: 1971 was the inaugural year. The races for 1971-82 were held on a two loop course around White Rock Lake, finishing at Winfrey Point. In 1983, the race was moved from Saturday to Sunday and the start/finish were at City Hall Plaza in downtown Dallas with the course making a loop around White Rock Lake.  In 2017, Chandler Self (NY/USA) was the first woman across the finish after she collapsed a short distance before the finish and was helped to her feet and supported thru the finish line by another runner.  Self was not disqualified by the officials but her mark cannot be accepted.
